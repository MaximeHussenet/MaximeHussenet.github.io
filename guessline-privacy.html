GuessLine - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

GuessLine ("the App") is developed by Maxime Hussenet. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Stored on Your Device

GuessLine is a fully offline game. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server:

  • Game Progress: Level completion, star ratings, unlocked content, and hint usage.
  • Settings: Your in-app preferences.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display optional rewarded ads (watch an ad to earn hints). AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's privacy settings. See Google Ads Privacy.

Ads require an internet connection. The App functions fully offline without watching ads.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No location data
  • No camera, photos, or microphone access
  • No contacts or calendar data
  • No user accounts or login credentials
  • No multiplayer or network features

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at contact@hussenet.com.
